kenny's salad recipe

The Nigerian Fried chicken salad is the closet you can get to perfect!

Full of West African goodness. A serving represents a bowl of happiness

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • carbage, green pepper, carrots, green peas, white onions.

Tin food

  • laser baked beans, sweet corn, farrows grean beans.

Salad dressing

  • Jago mayonnaise, remia salad dressing.

Meat content

  • Deboned chicken laps and breasts will do

Preparations

Sort fresh vegetables and wash clean.

Cut vegetables into small crafty chunks.

I advice cut in bits -Well it doesn't matter as long as you just chop the damn thing ...LOL

Put the vegetables in the fridge to chill in waiting.

Sieve off the water in the green beans and pour it in a big prep bowl.

Follow up with the rest of the tin food in the same bowl as the green beans

In another bowl mix in the mayonnaise and remia 6tsp to 1 respectively. Mutiply in this pattern according to quantity. Keep to chill.

Cook chicken for 20 minutes. I advice you marinate the night before - You may want to use kitchen glory, cory powder, knorr, salt, bay leaves, garlic etc

Fry in shallow vegetable oil till it's evenly brown.

Slice chicken and put in bowl.

Here comes the magic!

  1. Mix the vegetables and the chicken together.
  2. Pour in the salad dressing mixture and turn vigorously
  3. Turn till you have a perfect mixture of all ingredients

As always serve chilled and you'd better spare some because you'll be needing it later.
