Tasty french toasts

Tasty toasts to kick start your day

What's better than a breakfast well served?

Help yourself to so morning treats. Just as the saying goes: "breakfast is everything" French toast might be the answer.

ingredients

  • Full cream Milk -preferably three crown
  • fresh Eggs
  • Some cinnamon. [Optional]
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt
  • Vanila flavour

Directions

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanila, cinnamon, and salt together in shalloew bowl
  2. Lightly butter or oil a grill and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until for 3 to 4 minutes on both sides

Viola!! Serve hot and enjoy every bite!
